About Me

Full Stack Web Developer experienced in graphic design, with a background in Life Sciences at McMaster University and a Full Stack Development certificate at the University of Toronto. Passionate about tackling programming challenges with skills in front-end development languages such as HTML and Javascript and responsive app design. Known for writing clean solutions to coding problems and good collaboration with team members. My goal is to build interesting apps that are easy to use, easy to look at, and creates a lasting impact on users. I recently worked on a team of four to build a single-page application which uses the National Pokedex database to create a simplified turn-based battle system. Excited to apply my skills in a fast-paced, goal-oriented, and quality-driven team to develop new, exciting, or useful applications on the web.

In my spare time I tend to play games, watch tutorial videos, or draw. I've spent a decent amount of time learning basic animation, 3D modeling, video editing.
